Habit

Welcome to Habit, a bingo-like game to help kids develop good habits. It's a weekly sticker-chart that encourages kids to do their chores, homework, and other tasks.

Each letter/column has a theme, like "sleep", "eating", "sharing", etc... with a simple task to do, like "Try a new food", "Share a toy", "Brush teeth twice a day", etc... Kids get one sticker a day for each task they complete.
